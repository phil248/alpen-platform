## Engagement architecture

This is a 12-month enterprise engagement structured around the science-to-practice bridge applied at scale. The technology is not the work; the operating-model redesign is the work. CCG runs both in parallel.

### Phase 1 - Diagnostic and design (months 1-3)

Stakeholder interviews, current-state mapping, and an evidence-grounded future-state design. Your steering committee accepts the designed program before we begin building.

**Deliverables:**
- Workforce cognitive-capital current-state report (quantified where data exists; qualitative where it does not)
- Designed program portfolio mapped to evidence-grade outcomes
- Measurement framework with baseline metrics
- Stakeholder map and change-management plan

**Phase gate:** Designed program accepted by your steering committee. If we cannot reach a yes, you walk away with the diagnostic and a refund of months 4-12 fees.

### Phase 2 - Pilot and instrumentation (months 4-6)

One business unit runs the designed program live. Measurement instruments are deployed. The change-management plan executes. We refine the program design based on what the pilot teaches.

**Deliverables:**
- Live pilot program in one named business unit
- Measurement infrastructure operating against baseline
- Iteration log and program refinement document
- Weekly pilot-status reports to executive sponsor

**Phase gate:** Pilot business unit hits {{deal.pilot_metric_target}} within {{deal.pilot_metric_window}}. If not, the pilot extends by 4 weeks at no charge before scaling.

### Phase 3 - Scale and capability transfer (months 7-12)

Wave-based rollout to remaining business units. Your team takes program operations. CCG shifts to scientific-advisor mode. The narrative connecting brain health to business outcomes gets institutionalized in your reporting cadence.

**Deliverables:**
- Phased rollout to {{deal.rollout_count}} additional business units
- Capability transfer to your designated program owners
- Annual brain-capital report (template + first instance) for your executive team and board
- Published internal narrative connecting workforce cognition to performance and retention outcomes
- 30-day post-engagement advisory window

**Phase gate:** Capability transfer signed off by your sponsor. CCG is on advisory standby past this point.

## The five pillars in this engagement

This engagement is grounded in CCG's five messaging pillars, applied to your context:

1. **The science-to-practice bridge** - every program element is sourced to peer-reviewed evidence, with the evidence quality named transparently
2. **The brain economy** - your workforce cognitive capital is treated as an asset on the organizational balance sheet, not as an expense or perk
3. **Organizational responsibility, not individual blame** - interventions target work design, not employee behavior change
4. **Health analytics as business intelligence** - the measurement framework is built first; the program is built around what we can measure
5. **Global credibility, local action** - the engagement draws on CCG's international policy work but produces actionable internal program design specific to your organization
