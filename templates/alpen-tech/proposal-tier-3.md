## Engagement structure

This is an organizational change engagement. The technology is the easy half; the operating-model redesign is the hard half. Both happen in parallel.

### Phase 1 — Discovery and design ({{deal.phase_1_weeks}} weeks)

**Workstreams (parallel):**

| Workstream | What | Who from your side |
|---|---|---|
| Current-state mapping | Workflow + decision-rights inventory | Process owners |
| Capability assessment | What your team can do, can't do, won't change | Department leads |
| Future-state design | Joint working sessions to design the new operating model | Steering committee |
| OCM strategy | Stakeholder map, comms plan, resistance protocol | HR + comms partner |

**Phase gate:** designed future-state document accepted by your steering committee. If we cannot get to a yes, you walk away with the discovery deliverable and a refund of the build phase.

### Phase 2 — Build and pilot ({{deal.phase_2_weeks}} weeks)

**Workstreams (parallel):**

| Workstream | What |
|---|---|
| Platform build | Alpen Platform configured to the future-state design |
| Pilot execution | One business unit runs the new operating model live |
| Adoption metrics | Weekly leading-indicator review |
| Communications | Cadence per the OCM plan; stakeholder updates |

**Phase gate:** pilot business unit hits {{deal.pilot_metric_target}} within {{deal.pilot_metric_window}}. If not, we extend the pilot phase by 2 weeks at no charge before scaling.

### Phase 3 — Scale and capability transfer ({{deal.phase_3_weeks}} weeks)

**Workstreams (parallel):**

| Workstream | What |
|---|---|
| Rollout | Wave-based rollout to remaining business units |
| Capability transfer | Your team takes operations; we shift to advisory |
| Measurement institutionalization | Your team owns the metrics dashboard |
| Closeout | Formal handoff with 30-day post-launch advisory |

**Phase gate:** capability transfer signed off by your sponsor. We are advisory-only past this point.

## OCM workstream (the differentiator)

Most AI consulting fails because the technology is fine but the change isn't managed. This engagement runs OCM in parallel from week 0:

- Stakeholder mapping and influence analysis (week 1)
- Communications cadence — executive sponsor updates, all-hands moments, team-level rollout briefings
- Adoption metrics framework — leading indicators per phase
- Resistance management protocols — named escalations, named decision-rights
- Post-launch capability transfer plan

The OCM workstream is led by {{deal.ocm_lead_name}}, with {{tenant.principal_name}} as engagement lead.
