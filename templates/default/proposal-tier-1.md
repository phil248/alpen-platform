## Engagement model

### Phase 1 — Discovery (week 0)

Joint working session to map your team's tool stack and identify the 5-8 highest-value workflows to configure first.

### Phase 2 — Installation (weeks 1-2)

Platform installed in your environment. OAuth connections wired to your tools. Standard skills installed and verified working against your data.

### Phase 3 — Configuration (weeks 2-3)

Skills tuned to your terminology, brand voice, and reporting templates. Agents configured to your org structure. Test runs against real workflows with your team.

### Phase 4 — Handoff (week 4)

Kickoff workshop. Runbook delivered. Subscription begins.
